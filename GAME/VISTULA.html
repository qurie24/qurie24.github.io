Vistula

Now direct your steps to the given place.

You've arrived at the Vistula River, the lifeblood of Warsaw and Poland. The city's history is inseparable from this river. The Vistula, though sometimes treacherous, has always been at the heart of Warsaw, fueling trade and the city's development.

Today, the Vistula faces many challenges, such as climate change, which impacts water levels. Recently, the river's depth was only 20 cm in some places—an alarming sign for future generations. Watch the nearby poles carefully, you will find how much (in cm) was the highest water level in history. This is your password!
