Laboratory

Today it is the of Central Agricultural Library, but long ago, it was the Museum of Industry and Agriculture, where Maria conducted her first experiments. In the physical laboratory, organized with the help of her cousin, she was finally able to immerse herself in research. Maria worked here from 1890 to 1891.

As you already know, in those days, opportunities for women to work in science were extremely limited. It was here that Maria, even before moving to Paris, began to discover her scientific passion.
