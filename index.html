Welcome to the Skłodowska Quantum Conference!

The Skłodowska Quantum Conference (QURIE) will gather leading experts in quantum science, focusing on groundbreaking fields such as quantum communication, cryptography, computation, machine learning, and quantum technologies. This conference is organized as part of the Marie Skłodowska-Curie project AppQInfo (No 956071) under the European Union’s Horizon 2020 program.

The conference is scheduled to take place from October 2nd to October 4th, 2024, at the Faculty of Mathematics, Informatics, and Mechanics, University of Warsaw, in Warsaw, Poland. Over the course of three days, attendees will have the opportunity to engage in intensive sessions and discussions that will cover both theoretical and practical aspects of quantum technologies.

Registration

Please complete your registration by 13th September at 5 PM CEST. The number of seats is limited; therefore, registrations will be processed in the order they are received.

Participants will be notified of their acceptance individually after registration.

Register Now

Participation in the event is free of charge.

Attendees can choose to contribute in one of the following ways:

  • Poster Presentation: bring a poster in the A0 size in portrait orientation
  • Oral Presentation: present a talk that is 30 minutes in length

    • During the registration process, you are required to submit an abstract of your work. The abstract should be a maximum of 300 words in plain text.
