Maria Skłodowska-Curie

The QURIE Skłodowska Quantum Conference brings together the world of quantum sciences and the legacy of one of the greatest figures in science – Maria Skłodowska–Curie. While she did not work directly with quantum mechanics, her name graces our event thanks to the European Marie Skłodowska–Curie Actions (Horizon 2020). Hosted in Warsaw, her hometown, this conference offers you the unique chance to delve into her extraordinary life, visit her family home, and walk the very paths she once did.
